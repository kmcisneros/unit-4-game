CRYSTALS COLLECTOR!

#:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

If you match your total score to the random number, you win!

If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

Each time the game starts the values of each crystal changes.
